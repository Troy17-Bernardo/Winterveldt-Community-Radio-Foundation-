Winterveldt Community Radio

Community-driven radio dedicated to empowering voices.

MOTTO

"Your Voice Matters"

VISION

To bring local content close to you, creating awareness of social issues and current affairs, showcasing local music, news, stories, & entertainment, while ensuring that our community's stories are told & heard.

MISSION

To empower & engage our community through informative, entertaining, & inclusive programming, while promoting local talent & amplifying the voices of our listeners.
